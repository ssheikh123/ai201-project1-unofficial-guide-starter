Additional Notes on Professor Sara Riazi

Students discussing Professor Sara Riazi frequently mentioned that she was relatively new to UIC at the time of the discussion.

Common topics mentioned by students:
- Lecture organization
- Assignment clarity
- Communication style
- Overall workload

Some students reported concerns about teaching effectiveness in lower-level courses, while others noted that reviews from advanced courses were limited because she was a newer faculty member.

Potential strengths mentioned:
- Knowledgeable in her research area
- Accessible during office hours
- Willing to answer student questions

Potential concerns mentioned:
- Lecture delivery
- Assignment explanations
- Course organization

Students often recommended checking recent reviews because experiences varied between courses and semesters.

Student Recommendations

Students suggested attending office hours whenever possible to clarify assignment expectations and course material. Several commenters noted that experiences can vary significantly between semesters because instructors may revise course content, assignments, and grading policies.

Students considering courses taught by Professor Riazi were encouraged to:
- Review recent course feedback.
- Speak with students who recently completed the course.
- Begin assignments early.
- Seek clarification when instructions are unclear.

As with many instructors, student opinions varied and experiences were not universally positive or negative.
